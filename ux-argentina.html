UX Argentina

Challenge

Empower designers and strengthen the community

Since

2018

Argentina’s first large-scale UX salary and work survey.

Back in 2018, UX professionals in Argentina had little clarity about salaries, tools, or work conditions. Talking about pay wasn’t easy, and reliable data didn’t exist.

We co-created UX Argentina to change that—starting small, with a survey built by and for designers, aiming to share knowledge openly and help each other make better career decisions.

The Goal:

  • Provide clear, local insights on compensation, tools, and work realities
  • Give designers data they could actually use
  • Build a space for connection and shared learning

The process

We kept things simple and community-driven

How we did it:

  • Asked designers which topics mattered most
  • Grouped questions to make the survey quick and easy to answer
  • Improved it every year thanks to people’s feedback
  • Kept it anonymous and open to get honest responses

The Survey

Our focus was on making it accessible:

Main characteristics

  • Easy to complete from any device
  • Only essential questions are mandatory
  • A feedback loop that made each new edition better than the last.

To Sum Up

Problem

  • • No reliable salary data for UX professionals in Argentina

  • • Missing information about tools, workplace culture, and mental health

  • • Salary talk was a taboo, leaving designers without references to negotiate

Solution

  • • A national, community-led survey mapping real salaries and work experiences

  • • Annual updates shaped by participant feedback

  • • Share results openly to build transparency, empower negotiations, and foster a stronger UX community

A COMMUNITY EMERGED

I co-created UX Argentina seven years ago, starting with the first national UX salary survey. What began as a way to understand our local industry has grown into a community where designers share knowledge, connect, and support each other’s growth

Community Pillars

Slack

Our Slack space brings together 5,000+ designers who ask questions, share their work, and help each other out every day. A friendly, low-pressure place to connect and learn from peers.

Social Channels

Join our 11,000+ member LinkedIn group to swap ideas, insights, and opportunities. We’re also on Instagram and TikTok, where you can discover and follow amazing people from the UX community.

Job Board

Launched in 2022, our job board makes it easy to find and share UX opportunities, connect with companies, and support each other’s career growth.
