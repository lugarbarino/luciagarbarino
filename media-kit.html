Media kit

Product

Influur

Challenge

Build a highly engaged professional community

Duration

1,5 months

Context

Influencers constantly need an up-to-date, professional media kit to pitch to brands. Traditionally, they pay designers to pull data and design the kit every month. This is time-consuming, expensive, and often outdated by the time it's shared.

Problems:

  • Manual and costly process to update media kits
  • Static documents that quickly become irrelevant
  • Lack of customization flexibility style and branding

Solution:

We designed a fully integrated media kit feature within the Influur app.

  • Real-time data updates directly from their social metrics.
  • 100% customizable: Color themes, fonts, and choose which data to show or hide.
  • Saves money on designers and hours spent on manual updates.

Impact

More downloads, less friction, creators pitch faster, look better, and stay longer.

35%

Increase in media kit downloads, helping creators land more opportunities.

0$

No cost for design or to update their data — full control, no extra budget.

15%

Rise in user retention thanks to a smoother, more professional workflow.

Next Steps

AI-powered agent

Dynamically tailor each Media Kit segment depending on who’s viewing it, and even let brands hire or request a collab directly from the kit. Your Media Kit becomes a living, intelligent sales agent.
